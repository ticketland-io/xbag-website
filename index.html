xBag: Your Decentralized Backpack

xBag is a game-changing platform that revolutionizes how Web3.0 communities onboard and manage their members. With xBag, we introduce a unique concept of compound NFTs known as "xBags".

Each xBag is a singular, composite digital asset holding a multitude of other NFTs. These individual NFTs aren't just decorative; they provide users with distinct access rights, roles, and perks within the community. This dynamic structure allows for a more engaging and customizable user experience.

What sets xBag apart is its fluidity. Each NFT held within an xBag, or 'drop', can be detached and traded freely on the secondary market. This not only sparks a dynamic participation but also cultivates a thriving digital asset economy.

Furthermore, xBag champions permissionless cross-community interactions. It allows for seamless integration and exchanges between different communities. For example, one team can airdrop or sell benefits and features to holders of xBags issued by another team. This ensures an interconnected ecosystem where communities can grow and interact with each other effortlessly.
